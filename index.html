Our Story

Born in Atlanta,
Forged in Fire

ATL Blade Co. was founded by Atlanta craftsmen who believed that a knife should be more than a tool — it should be a work of art. Every blade we create is a testament to the ancient traditions of Damascus steel forging, reimagined for the modern world.

We source only the finest high-carbon and stainless steels, hand-folding each blade through hundreds of layers to create the unmistakable Damascus wave pattern. Our handles are crafted from premium stabilized wood, G10, and natural bone.

Privacy Policy

Effective Date: January 1, 2026

ATL Blade Co. ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting the privacy and security of our customers. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your personal information when you visit our website or make a purchase.

Information We Collect

When you place an order or create an account with us, we may collect the following information:

  • Full name and shipping/billing address
  • Email address and phone number
  • Payment method (processed securely — we do not store full card details)
  • Order history and product preferences
  • IP address and browser type for security and analytics

How We Use Your Information

Your information is used exclusively to:

  • Process and fulfill your orders
  • Communicate order status and shipping updates
  • Send marketing emails — only if you have opted in
  • Improve our website and customer experience
  • Comply with legal obligations

Email Marketing

We may send promotional emails about new products, exclusive offers, and company news to customers who have subscribed. You may opt out at any time by clicking the "Unsubscribe" link in any email or by contacting us directly at hello@atlbladeco.com.

Data Sharing

ATL Blade Co. does not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. We may share data only with trusted service providers strictly to fulfill your order.

Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures including SSL/TLS encryption, secure storage, regular audits, and PCI-DSS compliant payment processing.

Cookies & Tracking

Our website uses cookies to enhance your browsing experience. You may disable cookies in your browser settings. We do not use third-party advertising trackers.

Your Rights

You have the right to request a copy of your data, request correction or deletion, opt out of marketing, and lodge complaints with your local data protection authority.
