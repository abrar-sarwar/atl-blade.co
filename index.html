Our Story

A new shop,
in Atlanta

ATL Blade Co. was founded by Zian Bhutta, an Atlanta native who spent his whole life in the city before deciding it was time to actually make something for the place that raised him, instead of just being from it. The shop is new, and right now it's just him at a small bench with a slowly growing group of people around the city who care about this kind of work. Knives ended up being the starting point because the craft is honest and the result is something you can hold in your hand.

Most of the catalog is hand-forged Damascus steel, the patterned blades you'll see on the pocket knives and the skinner, while the fixed blades and the karambit are powdercoated for hard outdoor use. Handles are pakkawood, camel bone, micarta, or resin, picked to suit whatever the knife is built for, and every piece comes off the bench a little different from the one before it.

Privacy Policy

Effective Date: January 1, 2026

At ATL Blade Co., we keep this simple. We only collect what we need to handcraft your order, ship it to you, and take care of returns or refunds when something isn't right. Nothing more.

What We Collect

When you place an order or message us through the contact form, we collect:

  • Your name and shipping address
  • Email address and phone number
  • Order details — the blade you bought, quantity, and any notes
  • Payment confirmation (we never see or store your card number — that goes straight to our payment processor)

How We Use It

  • To make and ship your knife
  • To send you order and shipping updates
  • To reply to messages you send us
  • To process returns and refunds

That's it. We don't use your information for anything else.

Returns & Refunds Privacy

When you request a return or refund, we'll need your order details and a way to reach you. We keep that information only as long as it takes to resolve the return and meet our basic recordkeeping. See our Return Policy for the full process.

Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or rent your information. We share it only with the carriers and payment processors required to deliver your order.

  • Opt out of any emails from us at any time
